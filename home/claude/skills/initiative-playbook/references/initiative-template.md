# Initiative: <Initiative Name>

> Stakeholder-readable PRD. Lead with outcomes and vision, not internal process. This markdown is canonical; the tracker mirrors it.

## Executive Summary
A concise 2 to 5 paragraph explanation of what is being built, why it matters, and the desired end state. Focus on outcomes, not implementation.

## Business Objective
What business goal does this initiative support? (e.g. increase adoption, improve onboarding, establish the brand, generate organic traffic, reduce support burden.)

## Problem Statement
What problem exists today? Describe the current state, pain points, constraints, and the risks of doing nothing.

## Vision / Desired Future State
The world after this initiative is complete: what exists, who uses it, what they can do, how the experience is different.
